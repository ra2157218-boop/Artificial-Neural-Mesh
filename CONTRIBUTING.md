## üß≠ Safety & Ethics

ANM has strict rules:

* No harmful outputs
* No autonomous self-modifying behavior
* No unsafe tool execution
* No training on private or unethical datasets
* All expansions require human approval

If your contribution could affect safety, please open a discussion first.
